FAQ

How fast will I receive my report?

Within 48 hours of submitting your intake form — often faster. We'll email it directly to you with a Google Doc or PDF you can share with your team.

Do I need to give you access to anything?

Nope. We only need your Google Business Profile URL (the link to your listing on Google Maps) and your website URL. No passwords, no logins, no admin access required.

What format is the report?

A structured PDF/Google Doc with a prioritized action list, screenshots where relevant, and plain-English explanations. No jargon, no filler — just what to fix and why.

Is this a subscription?

No. This is a one-time flat fee. Pay once, get your report, done. No retainer, no recurring billing, no hidden fees.

What if I'm not happy with the report?

We stand behind our work. If you feel the report didn't deliver actionable value, email us at aaron@410strategy.com and we'll make it right — no runaround.
